About Kathleen Repko

Bio

Kathleen is a proud graduate of the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park, New York. She worked in a number of Cleveland area restaurants and hotels before shifting to administrative professional roles.
She and husband Tom are happy to be the parents of Maddie and Caroline. Together they enjoy volunteering for Pelotonia and VeloSano, cycling events that raise funds for cancer research at The Ohio State University Comprehensive Cancer Center, and the Cleveland Clinic Taussig Cancer Center, respectively.
She is also an avid fan of music, baseball, and hockey.
